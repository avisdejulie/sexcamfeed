Adult webcam aggregators: what they are and how to use them

A webcam aggregator pulls live feeds from several platforms into one interface. Instead of checking Chaturbate, LiveJasmin and Stripchat separately, you see all available performers in one place.

SexCamFeed is one of the leading aggregators for adult live cams, with filters by category, country and performance type.

The main limitation of aggregators: transactions still happen on the source platform. If you want to tip or enter a private show, you'll be redirected. This is ideal for discovery but less convenient for regular users with favourite performers.
